1. Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is one of the most popular dogs in the world. They are friendly, intelligent, and great family pets.

Origin: Canada

Nature: Friendly & Playful

Lifespan: 10-12 years

2. German Shepherd

German Shepherds are brave and loyal dogs. They are commonly used in police and military services.

    Origin: Germany

    Nature: Intelligent & Protective

    Lifespan: 9-13 years

3. Golden Retriever

Golden Retrievers are gentle and loving dogs. They are excellent companions and therapy dogs.

Origin: Scotland

Nature: Gentle & Loyal

Lifespan: 10-12 years
